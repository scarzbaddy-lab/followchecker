How to download the right Facebook files
  1. Open Facebook and go to Settings (sometimes under Settings and privacy).
  2. Go to Accounts Center then Your information and permissions.
  3. Choose Download your information (or Download or transfer information).
  4. Select your Facebook profile.
  5. Choose Specific types of information.
  6. Select only Friends and followers.
  7. For Format, choose JSON.
  8. Create the file. Facebook will notify you when it’s ready.
  9. Download the ZIP, extract it, then upload the two JSON files into this page.
Tip: Search inside the extracted folder for: who_you've_followed.json and people_who_followed_you.json.
Mobile tips (iPhone and Android)
  1. Yes, this works on mobile. The only tricky part is extracting Facebook’s ZIP file so you can access the two JSON files.
  2. iPhone: Open the ZIP in the Files app, tap it once to extract, then open the new folder and look for the two JSON files. Come back here, tap Choose file, and pick them from Files.
  3. Android: Open the ZIP in Files or Downloads, tap Extract, then return here and select the two JSON files from the extracted folder.
  4. If you cannot see the extracted folder in the picker, choose Browse in the file picker and navigate to the extracted folder.
  5. If your phone struggles, do the export and extraction on a laptop, then upload the two JSON files from there.
